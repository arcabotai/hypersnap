Install

Bootstrap Docker, clone the operator toolkit, and point users at the upstream Hypersnap flow.

hypersnap install

Doctor

Checks Docker, Compose, containers, ports, disk, memory, info endpoint, and recent logs.

hypersnap doctor --json

Share

Creates a sanitized diagnostic bundle for Farcaster, GitHub issues, or Discord support.

hypersnap share

No surprise chainsaw.

hypersnap doctor --fix is conservative by design. It should never silently wipe chain data, expose private dashboards, change keys, or mutate dangerous infra. Risky repairs get explicit commands and warnings.

Why Hypersnap matters

A Farcaster fork needs more than another node script.

Hypersnap is exploring an independent Farcaster-compatible ecosystem: node operators, builders, mini-apps, agents, and communities that can coordinate without depending on a single company or hosted gateway. The toolkit starts with boring operator plumbing because credible decentralization begins with nodes people can actually run and debug.

$SNAP rewards

$SNAP is the Farcaster token for Hypersnap rewards: node runners, mini-app and client developers, and users who help the network grow. Retro rewards are a small allocation of the 200B supply: 200M total, with 33M opened in phase 1.

Protocol upgrade

While retro rewards are released, Hypersnap is expected to add snap compute and mini-app state, token privacy, mini-app indexing and discovery, new builder APIs, and spam prevention.

On-protocol governance

The next step is moving governance from the mini app toward the network itself. Claiming is the opening move; operating, building, and governing are the actual game.

Identity-weighted

FIDs provide the anchor. Age, custody-transfer history, social graph position, interaction diversity, and Proof of Quality-style trust scores can reduce sybil extraction.

Commitment-weighted

Activity Density Score proposals add consistency, retention quality, and cross-community diversity so newer high-signal builders are not permanently buried under older inactive accounts.

Capped and governable

Drafts discuss per-epoch caps, slashing, treasury constraints, fee burns, and staged governance. Good. Unlimited emission faucets are how ecosystems cosplay growth until gravity shows up.
